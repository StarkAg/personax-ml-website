About the Project

Project Goal

Develop comprehensive customer personas through advanced segmentation using RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) to enable targeted marketing strategies and improve customer lifetime value.

Machine Learning Approach

Utilized K-Means clustering algorithm combined with PCA (Principal Component Analysis) for dimensionality reduction and visualization, enabling clear customer segment identification and actionable business insights.
